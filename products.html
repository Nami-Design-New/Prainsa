Prainsa produces Pre-Stressed Beams in different sections (or profiles). This includes:

  • I section pre-stressed beams. They are lighter in weight and can span for as long as 30 meter. Sections (width and depth) vary with load and span.
  • Rectangular Pre-Stressed Beams.
  • Inverted T Pre-Stressed Beams.

Rectangular Reinforced Beams are also produced when the Span is generally short, and there is no need for pre-stressing.

Delta Beams are special pre-stressed beams that are suitable for roofing of large span facilities like warehouses and factories.
Clear span can reach 50 m.
